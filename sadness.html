Sadness

You may notice a heaviness in your chest or a lump in your throat. You might feel tired, both physically and emotionally, and less interested in being around others. Your thoughts might be dwelling on things that didn't go well or feelings of loss. You might find it hard to focus or feel motivated.
While it's important to acknowledge and process your feelings, try not to dwell in sadness for extended periods. Use this emotion as a signal to reflect on what's truly important to you. Engage in self-care activities and reach out to trusted friends or professionals when needed. Remember that sadness can enhance empathy and compassion, making you more attuned to others' emotions. Consider expressing your feelings through creative outlets like art or writing.
