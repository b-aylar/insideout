Anxiety

If you're feeling anxious right now, you might notice your heart racing and your breathing becoming shallow. Your mind may be buzzing with worries about the future or replaying past events. You might feel tense, restless, or find it hard to concentrate on the present moment. These feelings are valid and common.
Remember, anxiety is manageable. Try taking a few deep, slow breaths to calm your body. Ground yourself by focusing on your surroundings or engaging in a simple task.
