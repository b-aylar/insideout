1. How overwhelmed do you feel by your current academic workload? Rate from 1 (completely manageable) to 5 (drowning in assignments and deadlines).
2. How satisfied are you with your social connections on campus? Consider your feelings of belonging or isolation. Rate from 1 (feeling completely disconnected) to 5 (feeling deeply connected and supported)
3. Consider your present emotional state regarding your future career prospects. How optimistic or worried do you feel right now? Rate from 1 (very pessimistic about the future) to 5 (extremely confident and excited about opportunities)
4. How would you rate your overall emotional well-being, considering factors like your sleep quality, eating habits, and exercise routine? Rate from 1 (feeling physically and emotionally drained) to 5 (feeling energized and emotionally balanced)
5. Imagine that your crush just liked your 3-week old Instagram post. On a scale of 1 to 5, how intensely are you overthinking this? (1 being 'Meh, probably an accident' and 5 being 'Planning our wedding and naming our future pets')
